Limestone

Limestone is one of the most abundant and important minerals found in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KPK). It is widely used in cement manufacturing, construction, and industrial processes. Districts like Dera Ismail Khan, Nowshera, and Kohat have rich limestone reserves. Limestone forms when these minerals precipitate out of water containing dissolved calcium.
